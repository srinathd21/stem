Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for SSCET?

You can register online by clicking the "Apply Now" button on any page, or contact your school coordinator. Registration is free and limited to 2000 seats per zone.

Where is the exam conducted?

The exam is conducted at designated schools across Dharmapuri district (6 taluks). Exact venue details will be shared on the hall ticket.

When will I receive my hall ticket?

Hall tickets will be sent via email/SMS one week before the exam date. You can also contact our helpline for any issues.

Is there any registration fee?

No, SSCET registration is completely free for all eligible students from 7th, 8th, and 9th standards.

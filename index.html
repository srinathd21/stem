About SSCET

STEM Jr Scholarship Cum Entrance Test – nurturing future IIT & NEET aspirants.

SSCET is a prestigious scholarship exam conducted by STEM Jr Academy for talented school students. The purpose is to identify bright minds and support them in competitive exam preparation from an early age. Selected students get financial aid and recognition.

Why participate? Opportunity to win scholarship, early NEET/IIT-JEE prep, logical thinking & practical skills, talent recognition.

📖 Exam Structure

LEVEL 1

Written Test (OMR Sheet)

Objective type multiple-choice questions. OMR based evaluation. Tests aptitude, science, and logical reasoning.

Qualify for Level 2

LEVEL 2

Practical Test

Hands-on assessment for shortlisted students. Real application of concepts & problem-solving skills.

Final selection basis both levels

Exam conducted in schools on a scheduled day. Zonal centres across Dharmapuri district (6 taluks).

🏅 Scholarship & Rewards

₹1000 Per Month

Yearly ₹12,000 (June 2026 – June 2027)

Top 3 students from each standard (7th, 8th, 9th) in each school who score highest in both levels will be selected for the monthly scholarship.

Every selected student receives ₹1000 per month for one full academic year.

Additionally, all participants get early exposure to NEET, IIT-JEE pattern and improve analytical thinking.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students studying in 7th, 8th, 9th standard
  • Government, Private, CBSE Schools – All eligible
  • Only 2000 applications allowed per Zonal (Dharmapuri region)
  • Exam conducted on a particular day at respective schools

Areas Covered

Students from six taluks across Dharmapuri District can participate. Comprehensive coverage from rural to urban schools.

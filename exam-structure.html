Two-Level Assessment

Exam Structure: Level 1 + Level 2

SSCET is designed to evaluate both theoretical knowledge and practical application. Students who clear Level 1 advance to the hands-on Level 2 test.

1

LEVEL 1

Written Test (OMR Sheet)

Objective multiple-choice questions conducted via OMR sheet. This level tests conceptual clarity, speed, and accuracy.

Key Highlights:
  • Subjects: Science, Mathematics, Logical Reasoning
  • Total Questions: 100 (MCQs)
  • Duration: 90 Minutes
  • Marking: +4 for correct, -1 for incorrect
  • OMR sheet evaluation – instant score calculation
Selection: Top 40% students from Level 1 qualify for Level 2.
2

LEVEL 2

Practical Test

Hands-on assessment for shortlisted students. This level evaluates application of concepts, experimental skills, and innovative thinking.

Key Highlights:
  • Practical problem-solving tasks
  • Lab-based or model-based activities
  • Assessed by STEM experts
  • Real-world application of science & logic
  • Duration: 60 minutes per batch
Final Selection: Combined score of Level 1 (60%) + Level 2 (40%) determines top 3 students per standard per school.

Exam Process & Timeline

Step 1: Registration

Students register online via school or portal. Limited to 2000 applications per zone. Registration closes one week before exam.

Step 2: Hall Ticket Generation

Digital hall tickets sent to registered email/SMS with exam venue, date, and timing.

Step 3: Level 1 – Written OMR Exam

Conducted at designated school centres across Dharmapuri district (6 taluks). OMR answer sheets collected for evaluation.

Step 4: Result Declaration (Level 1)

Shortlisted students (top 40%) are notified via school & email to appear for Level 2.

Step 5: Level 2 – Practical Test

Hands-on practical assessment at STEM Jr Academy lab or partnered schools.

Step 6: Final Selection & Scholarship

Top 3 students from each standard (7th,8th,9th) in each school receive ₹1000/month scholarship from June 2026 – June 2027.

Topics & Syllabus

The SSCET syllabus is aligned with school curriculum (7th-9th) and competitive exam foundations. Emphasis on conceptual clarity and logical application.

  • Physics Basics
  • Chemistry Fundamentals
  • Biology & Life Science
  • Mathematics
  • Logical Reasoning
  • Data Interpretation
Areas Covered: Six taluks across Dharmapuri District – Dharmapuri, Palakkodu, Pennagaram, Harur, Pappireddipatti, Karimangalam.

Important Exam Guidelines

Hall Ticket Mandatory

Carry printed hall ticket and school ID card to the exam centre.

Reporting Time

Reach the venue 30 minutes before the scheduled time. Latecomers not permitted.

Prohibited Items

Mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, or any electronic devices are strictly prohibited.

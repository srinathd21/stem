Who Gets the Scholarship?

The top 3 students who score the highest marks in both levels (Level 1 + Level 2) from each standard in each school will be selected for the scholarship.

Per School Selection: Separate winners for 7th, 8th, and 9th standards.
Evaluation Criteria: Combined score of Written Test (Level 1) + Practical Test (Level 2).
Scholarship Tenure: 12 months — from June 2026 to June 2027.

How Are Students Selected?

The selection process is transparent and merit-based. Students who perform exceptionally in both levels of the SSCET exam are eligible for the monthly scholarship.

Step 1
Level 1 Written Exam (OMR): Minimum qualifying marks required to proceed to Level 2.
Step 2
Level 2 Practical Test: Hands-on assessment of scientific aptitude and logic.
Step 3
Final Merit List: Combined score (60% Level 1 + 40% Level 2) determines top 3 per standard per school.
Step 4
Scholarship Disbursement: Monthly ₹1000 credited to parent/student account from June 2026.

Scholarship FAQs

Is the scholarship renewable?

The scholarship is awarded for one academic year (June 2026 – June 2027). Students must re-appear for SSCET each year to renew.

How many students get the scholarship?

Top 3 students from each standard (7th, 8th, 9th) in each participating school will receive the scholarship. So multiple winners per school.

When will the scholarship money be credited?

The first installment will be credited in July 2026, followed by monthly payments until June 2027.

Is there any bond or agreement?

No. It's a pure merit-based scholarship with no further obligations.

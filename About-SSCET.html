What is SSCET?

SSCET (STEM Jr Scholarship Cum Entrance Test) is a scholarship exam conducted by STEM Jr Academy for talented school students.

The purpose of this exam is to identify bright students and support them in preparing for future competitive exams like NEET and IIT-JEE. We aim to nurture young minds from an early age by providing financial assistance, mentorship, and exposure to high-level problem-solving.
